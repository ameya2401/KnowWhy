# Vision

Create the organizational memory platform that every software team wishes they had.

KnowWhy should become the single place where teams understand:

- Why decisions were made
- How projects evolved
- Who worked on what
- Where knowledge exists
- What should happen next

# Principles

Every future feature should satisfy at least one of the following:

- Solves a real user problem.
- Improves organizational memory.
- Enhances AI quality.
- Improves developer experience.
- Supports business growth.

If it satisfies none of these, it should not be built.

---

# What Success Looks Like

KnowWhy succeeds if:

- Teams rely on it daily.
- Developers onboard faster.
- Knowledge is never lost.
- AI answers are trusted.
- Organizations make better decisions because of KnowWhy.

Revenue, funding, and popularity are secondary outcomes.

The primary success metric is solving a meaningful problem.

# Final Commitment

KnowWhy began as an idea to build a strong portfolio project.

Whether KnowWhy becomes a university project, an open-source platform, or a successful startup, the goal remains the same:

Build software that solves meaningful problems, teaches valuable engineering skills, and creates lasting impact.
